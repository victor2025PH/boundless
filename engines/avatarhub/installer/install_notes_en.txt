BOUNDLESS Studio - Setup Notes

BOUNDLESS Technology - Communication, Boundless.

You are installing the "Studio" series digital-human live suite:
  - FaceX  : AI face swap for images / video / live streaming
  - VoiceX : zero-shot voice cloning and real-time voice changing
  - LiveX  : live digital avatar with real-time face & voice swap

This installer is small (tens of MB) and only installs the console.
All AI runtime components are downloaded automatically on first launch.

First run after installation:
  1. Double-click "BOUNDLESS Studio" on your desktop.
  2. The app detects your GPU and recommends the right edition;
     click "Install" to continue.
  3. Downloads show progress, speed and ETA (about 11-35 GB depending
     on edition, one-time only).
  4. The console opens automatically when ready.

Requirements:
  - NVIDIA GPU (6 GB VRAM or more; more VRAM unlocks more features,
    a feature list for your machine is shown during setup).
  - Stable network connection and enough free disk space.
